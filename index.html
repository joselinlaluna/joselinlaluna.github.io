Joselin Michelle Vazquez

Joselin Michelle Vazquez is a self-taught artist who channels the weight of the world into her work, capturing the universal feelings that connect us all—whether acknowledged or not. Growing up in Las Vegas, a place she calls home, Joselin's art is deeply influenced by the thoughts, feelings, and actions of others. She views her creative process as a way to tap into the shared human experience, bringing forth ideas and emotions that resonate with the realities of life, both challenging and uplifting. Her work is shaped by her own life experiences, both joyful and difficult. These experiences feed into her intuitive process to portray those deep feeling and thoughts into the canvases. Joselin goal is not just to dwell in the pain of the past but to reflect on that pain and heal through it.
